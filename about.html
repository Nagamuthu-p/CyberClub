About Us

Vision

To continually educate Cyber awareness and keep abreast about the emerging technologies of cyberspace,to develop club into a model club across the globe.

Mission

  • To inculcate Cyber ethics and Cyber security practices among the club members.
  • To learn the latest trends and techniques in the Cyber world.
  • To improve the Cyber awareness among the rural people to march towards Digital India.
  • Preparing young minds towards Cyber security profession.

Objectives

  • To conduct technical training programme.
  • To help the students to master in all tools by hands-on sessions and different competition.
  • To educate the student community on the capabilities to prevent and respond to Cyber threats.
  • To help the students to implement security concepts in their academic projects.
  • To conduct Cyber aware program for rural people.
