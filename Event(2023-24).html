                        Seminar on Cyber Crimes - Impacting Youth
Name of Organizing Unit: Cyber club and Cyber Society of India
Venue: Smart Class Room - I
Date of Event: 16th November 2023
Timing: 1.30 pm to 4.00 pm
Topic: Seminar on Cyber Crimes - Impacting Youth
Speaker details:  
Mr. Balu swaminathan, Secretary, Cyber Society of India, Additional Superintendent of Police (Retd.) - Cyber Crime
Mr. V.N. Premanand, Vice President, Cyber Society of India.
Target Audience: Cyber club students
Attendance: 100
Brief description:
Cyber club in association with Cyber Society of India conducted a Seminar on Cyber Crimes - Impacting Youth. Mr. Balu swaminathan, Secretary, Cyber Society of India, Additional Superintendent of Police (Retd.) - Cyber Crime and Mr. V.N. Premanand, Vice President, Cyber Society of India graced the occasion and enlightened the young minds by creating awareness with real case studies. On this occasion, Mr. Rayavel, Captain/Cyber club & HoD / Cyber Security introduced SCOPE members and MAGIC members of both Sri Sairam Engineering College and Sri Sairam Institute of Technology to the club members.
